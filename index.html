Gender Based Violence

A cruel Imbalance

Acid attacks in India have a gendered component: Out of the total reported attacks at least 72 percent included at least one female victim, according to news reports.

The fear factor

Acid attacks are at an all-time high in India and are rising year after year, with 250–300 cases reported each year, whereas the "actual number could exceed 1,000, according to Acid Survivor's Trust International". Because of the fear of retaliation, only a small number of cases are reported. It can take up to ten years for an offender to face justice, and some still walk free.

About The Campaign

#JustEmpowerOne is a cause-based campaign dedicated to spreading awareness about the needs of an acid attack victim and survivor. It's an initiative to impel the society to show empathy and nudge every individual to chip in their bit for the larger cause.

Acid attack is a heinous crime, and what is disheartening to know is the sheer number of women who are victims of this. The number testifies to the biases against women! An attack that predominantly affects women—not just physically but mentally as well. While it is the most unfortunate thing to happen to anyone, it is even more traumatic to live in constant fear, so much for being the 'weaker sex'.

The purpose of the brand JOY has been to make quality skincare products at affordable prices. And what better way of manifesting our purpose than to develop a range of personal care products called JOY Sensitive for the hypersensitive skin of acid attack survivors because currently other than derma products there is no skincare precast at an affordable price point for this marginalised section of women.

While we were looking into the aspect of physical scar and the well-being of their skin we also felt that a step ahead is necessary to make a substantial difference. We tied up with the NGO Atijivi to further interact with acid attack victims and survivors to understand their needs.

Once started we surely couldn't stop there! To further fulfil the basic needs and empower them to stand on their own, we launched the #JustHireOne Campaign. It is an endeavour that provides financial freedom to an acid attack victim and survivor leading to an esteemed life!
